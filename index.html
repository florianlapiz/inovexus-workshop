Strategy & Stack

1
The Seed-to-Series-A Outbound Problem
Why founder hustle caps at 10 customers — and what has to change
3 min

The tactics that got you your first 10 customers won't scale to 100. This workshop is about the transition from founder-led sales to a systematic, multi-threaded outbound motion.

2
Persona Matrix + Relevance
At Seed you sell to one. Upmarket you navigate five.
4 min

The 5-persona matrix for multi-threaded deals (Champion, Economic Buyer, Technical Buyer, End User, Blocker) — plus a brief recap on relevance per persona: one message per stakeholder, not per account.

3
The 3 Expansion Vectors
Size, Vertical, Use-case — most founders only see one
4 min

Three directions to expand — not just upmarket. Decision framework for when each vector makes sense, plus the non-obvious move: test two vectors in parallel instead of committing to one.

4
Signal-Based Account Selection
Firmographics tell you who. Signals tell you when.
4 min

The four signal categories (Growth, Buying, Pain, Timing) plus the 3-signal rule for upmarket targeting: don't touch an account without three stacked signals. Shown tool-agnostic.

5
Pipeline Math & Cadence
Enterprise deals die in the gap between month 2 and month 4
4 min

The math reality across SMB, Mid-Market, and Enterprise — why less volume wins upmarket. Plus a 6-month cadence playbook for deals that don't close in week 2.

6
Stack Compression (Tool-Agnostic)
The tool landscape is being eaten by AI primitives
3 min

Two shifts most founders haven't priced in yet: Claude Code eating enrichment workflows, Exa.ai eating intent signal discovery. Composable stack beats monolithic SaaS.

Wrap-Up

Three Rules to Take Home

One for strategy. One for targeting. One for execution.

1
Strategy

Problem first. Tool second.

If you can't describe your customer's pain in their own words, no tool will save your outbound.

2
Targeting

Stack three signals.

Single-signal plays are crowded. Three signals give you a timing window only you see.

3
Execution

Multi-thread from day one.

At €25K+ ACV, one-contact outreach caps your pipeline. Five personas per account is the new one.
